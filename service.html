Private Limited Company

Overview: India's most trusted legal structure for startups. Offers limited liability protection.

Eligibility:

Min 2 directors (one resident in India), min 2 shareholders.

Documents:

PAN, [Aadhaar Redacted], Identity proof, Utility bill, Rent agreement/NOC.

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

Overview: Ideal for professionals wanting a flexible partnership with limited liability.

Eligibility:

Min 2 partners, at least one designated partner must be an Indian resident.

One Person Company (OPC)

Overview: Full control for solo entrepreneurs with limited liability protection.

Eligibility:

1 Indian resident as Director/Shareholder, must appoint a nominee.

Partnership Firm

Overview: Simple and economical structure for family-run or small trades.

Eligibility:

Min 2 partners (Max 50), requires a written Partnership Deed.

GST Registration

Overview: Crucial for professional credibility and operating across state borders.

MSME / Udyam Registration

Overview: Unlocks government benefits, subsidies, and lower interest rates on loans.
