Private Limited Company

Overview: A separate legal entity offering limited liability, ideal for startups seeking investor funding.

Eligibility:

Minimum 2 directors, 1 resident in India, No minimum capital.

Documents:

PAN, Aadhaar [Redacted], Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Photo.

One Person Company (OPC)

Overview: A structure where one person has full control with limited liability protection.

Eligibility:

Single shareholder/director, must appoint a nominee, must be an Indian resident.

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

Overview: A flexible partnership structure offering limited liability protection.

Eligibility:

Minimum 2 partners, no minimum capital, registered office in India.

GST Registration

Essential for tax compliance for businesses exceeding turnover thresholds.

MSME / Udyam Registration

Unlock government benefits and subsidies for small and medium enterprises.
