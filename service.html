Our Legal Services

Private Limited Company

Overview: The best corporate identity for startups. It provides limited liability protection and builds massive trust for funding. We handle the entire MCA filing process from A to Z, ensuring 100% compliance.

Eligibility:

Min 2 Directors, Min 2 Shareholders, Indian Resident Director required.

Registration Process:

1. Name Search 2. DSC Procurement 3. MoA/AoA Drafting 4. SPICe+ Filing 5. COI Issuance.

Documents Required:

PAN, Aadhaar, Utility Bill (Electricity), Rent Agreement.

How We Help:

We provide a dedicated manager, handle DIN/DSC, and assist in current account opening.

GST Registration

Overview: Mandatory for businesses exceeding turnover limits. We ensure your business is tax-compliant and eligible for government tenders.

Includes:

Registration, Monthly Filing (GSTR-1, 3B), and Annual Returns.

LLP Registration

Overview: A perfect blend of partnership flexibility and corporate limited liability. Ideal for professional services.

Process:

DSC Procurement -> Name Reservation -> Incorporation -> LLP Agreement Filing.

OPC (One Person Company)

Overview: Corporate status for solo entrepreneurs. You control the company, but with the benefits of a Private Limited entity.

MSME/Udyam Certificate

Overview: Get official government recognition to avail subsidies, lower interest rates on loans, and priority sector lending.

Startup India Registration

Overview: DPIIT recognition to get tax holidays and direct access to government funding programs.
